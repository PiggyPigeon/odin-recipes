Yogurt

This is a super simple and healthy recipe. It only requires a spoon.

Ingredients

  • Tub of yogurt.
  • Spoon.
  • *Optional* bowl & fixins.

Instructions

  1. Take yogurt tub out of mom's fridge.
  2. Employ spoon to eat yogurt.
  3. Oh, yea, get a bowl if mom is around.
  4. Option to add fixins', ex:frozen berries, mom's special protein powder, your step dad's special cereal.

Cottage Cheese

This is another versatile, affordable, and healthy super-recipe. It requires a spoon, sometimes. Sometimes you may also want a bowl. I make a stand and ask mom to only buy Daisy Brand cottage cheese because on this one fucking thing I decided to pretend to care about 'emulsifiers'.

Ingredients

  • One tub of Daisy Brand cottage cheese (bigger=better).
  • *Optional* bowl.
  • *Optional* spoon. If you get hungry at the grocery store or on the road you can fold the lid of the cheese tub and use it as a scoop.

Instructions

  1. Open tub of cheese.
  2. Use spoon (or folded lid) to eat cheese.
  3. That's it!
